Hi! I’m Lasse and I have a great passion for technological engineering.

I expect to graduate in the summer of 2024, and, in combination with my bachelor's degree (BSc.) in Software Technology, I will receive a Master of Science in Engineering degree (cand.polyt).

With DTU’s high ambitions and high expectations towards students and their dedication, this is a perfect match for me. I have always had high ambitions, and I have never been afraid of taking up a challenge that could potentially broaden my skill set. With this in mind, it is indeed very helpful, that I am also skilled at structuring and managing tasks and deadlines, such that I get to carry out most of the things I set my mind to.

Going back in time, once again, I graduated from high school in 2018. With my (undocumented) skill set within technological engineering and my high-school diploma, I managed to be hired as a full-time programmer on equal terms with my (slightly more experienced) coworkers at Morning Train, an Odense-based web agency, on the basis of my skills. I worked there for a year after which began my studies at Technical University of Denmark in Kongens Lyngby.

Even though I started studying at a place far away from the office of Morning Train, I continued my partnership with the organization, as I worked remotely for their sister-company, Morningscore, for the almost two years to follow while managing my studies.
